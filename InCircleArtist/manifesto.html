Artist Manifesto

The person who stands in the circle is an artist.


Behaviour

The person who stands on the shoulders of giants is an artist.

The person who doesn't steal other people's ideas is an artist.

The person who has a professional artist statement is an artist.

The person who has extremely high demands on themselves is an artist.

The person who allows themselves to fail again is an artist.

The person who knows how to resist is an artist.

The person whose life does not repeat is artist.

The person whose itinerary is not two-point-one-line is an artist.

The person who has tasted bittersweet and sour is an artist.


Personality

The person who has consistent faith is an artist.

The person who often struggle with their hearts is an artist.

The person who likes to be alone anytime is an artist.

The person who will not compromise their lives is an artist.

The person who cannot lie is an artist.

The person who is both lonely and fulfilled is an artist.

The person who knows their strengths at all times is an artist.

The person who always knows that they are inadequate is an artist.

The person who is humble but not accept to other’s idea is an artist.


Artworks/Ideas

The person whose work is their baby is an artist.

The person who is not satisfied with the state of the world is an artist.

The person who knows who they are is an artist.

The person who knows what they're doing is an artist.

The person who can clearly express their views of the world is an artist.

The person who has their own cosmology is an artist.

The person who creates spontaneously instead of following the trends is an artist.

The person who can identify artists is an artist.

The person who often think the exact opposite is an artist.


Fame and Wealth

The person who has a name or stage name is an artist.

The person who is known to the public online and offline is an artist.

The person who cannot defeated by interests is an artist.

The person who sees their fame as a creation is an artist.

The person who sees money games as creations is an artist.

The person who earns wealth by both brain power and hands-on ability is an artist.

The person who doesn't care about being an artist is an artist.

The person who acknowledges themselves as an artist is an artist.

The person who always keep distance themselves from fame and fortune is an artist.


…

The person who implements earnestly the manifesto is an artist.
